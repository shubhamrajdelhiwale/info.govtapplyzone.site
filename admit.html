What is an Admit Card?

Admit Card is an official entry ticket issued by the exam conducting authority. It contains your name, roll number, photograph, exam date, exam city, reporting time and important exam day instructions. Without an admit card, you will not be allowed to enter the exam hall.

Always download your admit card from the official website of the respective exam authority. Beware of fake or unauthorized download links.

How to Download Admit Card

Follow these simple steps to download your admit card from the official website:

  • Visit the official exam conducting authority website.
  • Find the “Admit Card” or “Hall Ticket” section.
  • Enter your Registration Number and DOB / Password.
  • Verify your details and click on Download.
  • Take a printout for exam day.

Always carry a valid photo ID (Aadhaar / Voter ID / Passport) along with your admit card.

Documents to Carry on Exam Day

  • Printed Admit Card
  • Valid Photo ID Proof
  • Pen & Pencil (if allowed)
  • Passport size photograph (if mentioned)

Important Instructions Before Exam

Read all the instructions mentioned on the admit card carefully. Reach the exam center at least 1 hour before the reporting time. Keep the admit card safe as it will be required during document verification.

Preparation Tips After Admit Card

Focus Your Revision

Now that your exam date is fixed, revise all important subjects and topics. Focus on your weaker sections.

Practice Mock Tests

Take mock tests regularly to improve speed and accuracy before the actual exam.

Check Exam Details

Double-check your exam city, time, seating arrangement and reporting instructions.
