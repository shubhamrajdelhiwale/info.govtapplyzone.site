Exam City, Slot Booking & Admit Card

SSC will release the exam city intimation slip first. Slot booking opens from 15 January 2026 to confirm date and shift. Admit cards are released region-wise 4–7 days before the exam and must be carried in printed form with a valid photo ID.

Preparation Strategy

Revise arithmetic basics, reasoning fundamentals, English grammar and comprehension, and General Awareness. Practice mock tests and previous year papers regularly. Accuracy is crucial in Session II due to negative marking.

After CBT – What Next?

SSC will release the provisional answer key followed by an objection window. After review, the final answer key and results will be declared. Havaldar candidates qualifying CBT will appear for PET/PST. Final selection is based on CBT performance, PET/PST (for Havaldar), and document verification.
