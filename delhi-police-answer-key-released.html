Delhi Police Constable Eligibility Criteria

Educational Qualification

Candidates must have passed 10+2 (Senior Secondary) examination from a recognized board. Higher educational qualification does not give any additional weightage.

Age Limit

The age limit for Delhi Police Constable is generally between 18 to 25 years. Age relaxation is applicable for SC, ST, OBC, Ex-Servicemen and other reserved categories as per government rules.

Delhi Police Constable Selection Process

The selection process for Delhi Police Constable consists of multiple stages. Candidates must qualify each stage to proceed further in the recruitment process.

  • Computer Based Examination (CBE)
  • Physical Endurance Test (PET)
  • Physical Measurement Test (PMT)
  • Document Verification
  • Medical Examination

Delhi Police Constable Syllabus

The syllabus for the Delhi Police exam includes General Knowledge, Current Affairs, Reasoning, Numerical Ability and Computer Awareness. Questions are generally of 10+2 level.

General Knowledge includes history, geography, polity, economics, science and current affairs. Reasoning tests logical thinking, while numerical ability includes basic arithmetic.
