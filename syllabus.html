Exam Syllabus 2025 – Complete Govt Exam Patterns

View detailed syllabus and exam patterns for all major Sarkari Exams in 2025, including SSC, Railway, Banking, UPSC, State PSC, Teaching and Defence exams.

Why Exam Syllabus Is Important

The syllabus is your roadmap for exam preparation. It tells you exactly what topics will be covered and helps you plan your study strategy effectively. Understanding the syllabus and exam pattern increases your chances of success.

SSC Syllabus 2025

SSC CGL & CHSL Pattern

Staff Selection Commission exams have different tiers including objective and descriptive sections. Below are the major subjects:

  • General Intelligence & Reasoning
  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • English Language
  • General Awareness

Tier-II / Mains papers include Quantitative Abilities, English Language and Statistics/Finance (where applicable).

Railway Syllabus 2025

RRB NTPC & Group D Pattern

The Railway Recruitment Board exams test candidates on reasoning, math, and general awareness.

  • Arithmetic / Mathematics
  • General Intelligence & Reasoning
  • General Awareness

Banking Exam Syllabus

IBPS / SBI / RBI Pattern

Banking exams have multiple phases including preliminary and main exams.

  • English Language
  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • Reasoning Ability
  • General Awareness with Banking Awareness

UPSC Syllabus 2025

Civil Services Exam Pattern

The UPSC Civil Services Examination has three stages: Prelims, Mains, and Interview. Important areas include:

  • History & Culture
  • Polity & Governance
  • Geography
  • Economy & Environment

State PSC Syllabus & Pattern

State Public Service Commissions conduct exams like UPPSC, MPPSC, BPSC, and RPSC. Each state has its own syllabus but core subjects remain:

  • General Studies
  • Current Affairs
  • General Science & Reasoning
  • Optional / Domain Subjects

Teaching & TET Exam Syllabus

CTET / State TET Pattern

Teacher Eligibility Tests (TETs) focus on child development, pedagogy, and subject knowledge.

  • Child Development & Pedagogy
  • Language I & II
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental Studies

Defence Exam Syllabus

NDA / CDS Pattern

Defence entrance exams test both academic and general awareness skills.

  • Mathematics
  • General Ability Test
  • English
  • General Knowledge & Current Affairs

Study Planning Tips

After knowing the syllabus, organize your preparation strategy. Start with basic concepts, revise regularly and solve previous year papers.

  • Understand exam pattern
  • Make a study timetable
  • Practice mock tests regularly
  • Focus on core subject areas
