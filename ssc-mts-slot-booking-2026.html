SSC MTS 2025 Exam Dates Announced

Staff Selection Commission has announced the SSC MTS 2025 exam dates. As per the official notice dated 2 January 2026, the Multi-Tasking (Non-Technical) Staff and Havaldar (CBIC & CBN) Examination 2025 will commence from 4 February 2026. The self-slot booking process will begin from 15 January 2026. Admit card, exam city intimation slip and further updates will be released on the official website ssc.gov.in.

SSC MTS 2026 Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must be Indian citizens (or eligible categories). Educational qualification is Matriculation (Class 10). Age limit is 18–25 years for MTS and 18–27 years for Havaldar posts, with age relaxation applicable for reserved categories.

SSC MTS Selection Process

  • Computer-Based Examination (CBE)
  • PET / PST (for Havaldar only)
  • Document Verification

SSC MTS Exam Pattern 2026

CBT consists of two sessions conducted on the same day. Session I has no negative marking. Session II includes negative marking of 1 mark per wrong answer. Each session is of 45 minutes.

SSC MTS Syllabus 2026

The syllabus includes General Intelligence & Reasoning, Numerical Aptitude, English Language and General Awareness. Questions test basic reasoning, arithmetic, grammar, vocabulary and current affairs.

SSC MTS Salary 2026

SSC MTS and Havaldar posts are Pay Level-1 as per the 7th Pay Commission. These are General Central Service, Non-Gazetted, Non-Ministerial posts.

SSC MTS 2026 Preparation Strategy

Candidates should focus on syllabus-based preparation, practise mock tests and previous years’ papers, revise current affairs and maintain accuracy, especially in Session II due to negative marking.
